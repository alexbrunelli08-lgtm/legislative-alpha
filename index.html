How this works

01 — Key bills, by sector

The most important bills moving through each sector — ranked by how far they've advanced, whether they appropriate funding, and momentum. Open any bill to see the stocks positioned to benefit and the congressional trades disclosed in exactly those tickers.

02 — Follow a politician

Every member's disclosed trades, aggregated into a portfolio you can open and inspect — top tickers, buy/sell split, sectors they're active in. The disclosure, reorganized around the person, the way copy-trading apps frame it.

03 — What Congress is trading

A consensus signal per stock: how many distinct members traded each ticker and the net buy/sell direction across Congress — surfacing which names are drawing the most collective activity right now.

About this data. Bills from the Congress.gov API; trades from Senate and House STOCK Act disclosures (Senate candidates and House paper filings are excluded). "Stocks that benefit" is a sector mapping, not a prediction, and nothing here estimates returns. Sector and company lists live in scripts/sectors.json. Refreshes daily. Not investment advice.
